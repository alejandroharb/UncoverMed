UncoverMed

In 2015, I declined offers to attend medical school. I spent 2 years before my decision ambivalent about entering medicine. One year into my decision, I was still doubtful. Finding direction took effort and time. I found my journey difficult because of the complexity of healthcare and the decentralized discussion of important topics.

My curiosity led me to unwrapping healthcare. I had countless discussions with experts in fields like medicine, pharmaceutics, microbiology, healthcare economics, and healthcare technological management. I pursued different fields like economics, entrepreneurship, and technology.

UncoverMed aims to present and express the topics of Medicine that are often overlooked. The goal is to not only share my experience and that of others, but to also contribute to making the complexity digestible and understandable. I aim to rightfully express the beauty in the complexity and the audacious attempts at solutions.
